Welcome to the home of the Edinburgh SIAM & IMA Student Chapter!

We are the joint student chapter of the Society of Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM) and Institute of Mathematics and its Applications (IMA) at the University of Edinburgh and Heriot-Watt University.

We are run by a committee of PhD students from the the School of Maths at Edinburgh and the Departments of Maths and Actuarial Sciences, and School of GeoEnergy Engineering at Heriot-Watt. Membership is open to anyone in maths or a related discipline at either university. Our aim is to connect members of these departments who use any kind of maths (not only applied!) in their research.
